About me

Creative Art Designer
And Web Developer

Hey there! My name is Sebastian Roervig. I hailed from Denmark and went to university in the US. I started my career in Silicon Valley and then moved to Asia (Philippines, Pakistan, and Singapore. I always worked in Fintech and within fintech, payments. I just left Grab, where I spent the last three and a half years building out the financial service arm to start my own payments company. Payments are often rather complicated in Asia with too many options to choose from, so our mission is to make it simple but combining everything into a simple one-click payment across the internet. From a personal side, besides loving to travel and anything that combined spicy food, given I worked in tech and come from a non-technical background, I am now in the midst of taking a Professional Certificate in Coding: Full Stack Development with MERN - December 2020 from MIT. I hope that it will be the first part of a long journey to develop a more substantial technical appreciation and understand to continue to accel my technical career.
